## Numbered + emoji-prefix corporate structure

A starter structure tailored for compliance / corporate / multi-team
contexts (numbered-PARA variant with explicit Tasks, Topics, Knowledge,
and Sandbox layers):

```
📥 DROPZONE, Inbox
🔎 Index, Navigator, Map, MOCs
0 - Tasks, Planning
1 - Workspaces, Projects
2 - Areas, Initiatives
3 - Docs, Internal Info, SOPs
4 - Topics, Knowledge, External
5 - Archive, Admin
6 - Ideation, Sandbox
7 - Vault Config & Management
99 - ARCHIVE
📄 WELCOME - Wiki Usage
```

## Older notes — components for building your own system

> Use these like Lego blocks under an ontology, top level, or wherever works
> best. Most are oriented towards knowledge work — great for action and
> project-oriented work. The short-term and long-term action relations are the
> hardest part to manage in these systems. You can throw everything into the
> big archive or use an archive per folder.

### ACE

- **A**tlas — MOCs and mapping to things — ontologies.
- **C**alendar — time-based.
- **E**fforts — action-oriented.

### e-ACCESS — knowledge at top, action at bottom

- **E**ncounters (cooling pad, unsorted).
- **A** — Atlas — MOCs, dashboards, directories, overviews, consumption.
- **C** — Calendar — daily, meetings, journals.
- **C** — Cards — ideas, things, concepts, statements, atomic.
- **E** — Extras — support materials and images.
- **S** — Sources — clippings, articles, sources.
- **S** — Spaces — areas of life. Spaces have their own PARAs or other organizational structure.

### ACE (variant)

- **A** — Atlas.
- **C** — Calendar.
- **E** — Efforts.

### PARA

- **P**rojects.
- **A**reas.
- **R**esources.
- **A**rchive.

### Flat with naming scheme

- Only good top-level if you just need your system for knowledge work and not
  integration with other systems or for external consumption.
- This is risky — you may hit some bottlenecks in the workflow.
- If you have a really good naming system that uses dates or an ID or naming
  code system at the front, then this can be doable.
- Not good if you need to access outside of Obsidian a lot or have other
  people look through it.

### Time-based with naming scheme

- 4 folders — *now*, *soon*, *later*, *archive*.
- File names like `year-month-topic-keyword`.
